Music

As a music lover, putting on my favourite songs on a calm, carefree afternoon is the absolute best. Many people even think that music understands them better than anyone else could.

Besides listening to music however, I have also picked up a hobby of playing the ukulele. It was something I found an interest in from our music lessons in school. I don't usually play instruments, so sometimes I would just find my instrument, play a few notes according to a chord sheet online and put it back.

Music does help me relax during stressful times, making it one of my favourite pastimes.
